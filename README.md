## The Spice Melange

He who controls the Spice, controls the universe. Found only on the deep desert planet of Arrakis, the spice extends life, expands consciousness, and makes safe interstellar folding of space possible. 

> *"The concept of progress acts as a protective mechanism to shield us from the terrors of the future."* — Frank Herbert

---

### The Great Houses

The political landscape of the Known Universe is balanced on the edge of a knife, governed by the Padishah Emperor, the Spacing Guild, and the Landsraad.

* **House Atreides:** Hailing from the water-rich world of Caladan. Known for their nobility, honor, and dangerous popularity.
* **House Harkonnen:** The brutal, industrialized masters of Giedi Prime. Wealthy, ruthless, and driven by centuries of vendetta.
* **House Corrino:** The Imperial House, ruling from Kaitain, backed by the terrifying military might of the Sardaukar.

### The Ecology of Arrakis

To understand Dune is to understand its environment. It is a world engineered by its own apex predators.

1.  **Shai-Hulud (The Sandworms):** Massive, territorial beasts that roam the deep desert. They are the creators and protectors of the spice.
2.  **Fremen:** The indigenous inhabitants of Arrakis. Masters of desert survival, driven by a fierce religious fanaticism and a dream of a green Arrakis.
3.  **Water Discipline:** In the deep desert, a man's flesh is his own, but his water belongs to the tribe. Stillsuits capture and recycle the body's moisture to sustain life in the wasteland.
